Bayan Park

Aurora Hill, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Description:

Bayan Park is a revitalized community park in Baguio City, featuring a playground, calisthenics area, picnic grounds, skatepark, jogging paths, and an amphitheater.

What to expect in Bayan Park:

  • A sunken children’s playground.

  • An outdoor gym / calisthenics zone and jogging paths.

  • A skatepark / skateboarding area

  • Family-friendly picnic grounds

  • A community amphitheater for events and gatherings.

Burnham Park

Jose Abad Santos Dr, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Description:

The Baguio City: Burnham Park is one of the most iconic and beloved attractions tourism in the City. Named after American architect, Daniel Burnham, who built and designed the city's layout, this park offers tourists a scenic spot for picnics, boat rides, biking, and relaxing strolls in the beautiful gardens.

What to expect in Burnham Park:

  • Burnham Lake – A man-made lake at the center of the park.

  • Gardens & Green Spaces

  • Event & Sports Grounds

  • Relaxation, Picnics & People-Watching

Mines-Veiw Park

Description:

Nestled in the heart of Baguio City, Mines View Park is a gem that promises a mix of breathtaking views, cultural immersion, and a relaxing escape from the hustle and bustle. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or a first-time visitor, this iconic destination is sure to captivate you. Famous for its panoramic views of the Benguet mountains, Mines View Park showcases the lush greenery and distant traces of abandoned gold and copper mines, making it a visual treat. Beyond the views, the park offers a rich cultural experience where you can interact with locals, try on traditional Mines View Park Igorot costumes, and shop for unique souvenirs. Located at Mines View Park Baguio City, the park is easily accessible from major landmarks.

CJ9G+VQ9, Baguio, Benguet

Wrigth Park

Description:

Wright Park is a wooded area in Baguio which became known for its horseback riding services for tourists. It was named after American Governor Luke E. Wright and was established as a recreational area.It is situated near The Mansion. The horses are owned and handled by "pony boys", who are often a member of the Ibaloi community. The park was closed down in early 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but has since reopened to local tourists in July 2020. In the same year, an outbreak of equine infectious anemia broke out affecting the health of horses in Wright Park. In September 2020, the Baguio city government unveiled a ₱717 million development plan which it conceived prior to the pandemic.

The Mansion, Romulo Dr, Baguio, Benguet

Bamboo Sanctuary

Description:

Need a break? then we recommend Bamboo sanctuary,This serene attraction features beautifully arranged bamboo pathways, towering groves, and picture-perfect trails that immerse visitors in cool mountain air and natural greenery.
